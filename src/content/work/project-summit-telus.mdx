## Context

Project Summit is the follow-on contract to [TechCentral](/work/techcentral-telus): a complete overhaul of how TELUS trains, mentors, and certifies its field-services technicians. Where TechCentral solved "where does information live," Summit solves "how do people grow inside this organization."

## What's being built

A learning platform with three intertwined surfaces:

- **Training.** Structured paths for new hires, role transitions, and continuing education — modular, trackable, and tied into the same identity and role model as TechCentral.
- **Mentorship.** Pairing senior technicians with newer ones in a way that's visible to both, scaffolded by the platform but driven by the people in it.
- **Certification.** Credential issuance, expiry, and audit — what a technician is qualified to do, when it was verified, and what's coming up for renewal.
